▸ Hello! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Clark University.
▸ My research focuses on urban economics and international trade, specifically on the determinants of the spatial distribution of economic activities in China using general equilibrium models.
▸ I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Economics from Shanghai Ocean University in 2018 and participated in a joint graduate program at University of Toronto, funded by Shanghai University, from 2019 to 2020. I hold Master’s degrees from Shanghai University (2021) and Clark University (2023).
